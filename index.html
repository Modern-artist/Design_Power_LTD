Our Services

At Design Power, we offer a range of solar permit design and proposal layout services that are tailored to meet the unique needs of your residential or commercial solar energy project. Our team of experienced engineers, designers, and proposal specialists is dedicated to helping you obtain the necessary permits and win more projects for your solar energy business.

Permit Documentation:

We prepare all necessary permit documentation for your solar energy project, ensuring that it meets all local and state regulations. Our team is familiar with the permit requirements in various jurisdictions across the United States, and we work closely with the relevant authorities to ensure a smooth permitting process.

Proposal Layout:

We offer a range of proposal layout solutions that can help you win more business. Our team specializes in creating custom proposal layouts that are professionally designed, expertly written, and eye-catching. We work with you to ensure that your proposal meets your specific needs and exceeds your expectations.

Site Analysis:

We conduct a thorough analysis of your property to determine the best location for solar panel installation. Our team considers factors such as the roof orientation, shading, and other obstructions to ensure that your solar energy system is designed to maximize energy production.

System Design:

We collaborate with you to create a solar energy system that fulfils your unique requirements and goals. Our team utilizes the latest technologies and software to create a comprehensive system design that is both efficient and cost-effective.
